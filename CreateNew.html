How to use this Site?

This is where you can create a new pixel design on what you want to make. Soon you can be able to save the design and/or upload the design you created but for right now you should screenshot your image because if you exit out or refresh this page with the design it will reset to a clean slate. So make sure you screenshot the design and paste on google docor some other site you desire to put your design on to save it.

How to use the pallets: The pallete is the multiple colors above the white table use to make the design, All you need to do is to click on the colors you desire to use on the white table, and click on the white square you want to place it on. If you mistakenly place a color you didn't want on your design, you can click on the white square on the multicolor pallette to delete the desired square on the table, or just click on a different color on the pallete to replace the color
